
# Heretic Warband


## Leader / Lieutenant

1 Heretic Lieutenant — 120 ducats

Weapon: Automatic Pistol

Melee weapon: Sword/Axe

Equipment: Trench Shield, Combat Helmet, Standard Armour
(Set-up to be a solid “anchor” figure with decent gear)


## Elites

1 Heretic Priest (Sniper) — 60 ducats

Weapon: Bolt-Action Rifle
(Provides long-range threat and support)


## Troops / Core Infantry

3 Yeomen — 4 × 35 ducats = 140 ducats

Weapon: Bolt-Action Rifle each
(Cheap core troopers to fill out the list and soak actions)

2 Shocktroopers — 2 × 75 ducats ≈ 150 ducats

Each: Pistol + Greatsword or Greataxe, Standard Armour
(More durable, flexible melee units)

1 Mechanised Heavy Infantry (MHI) — 130 ducats

Weapon: Machine Gun

Equipment: Machine Armour
(Heavy-hitter unit, leverages stronger kit)


## Misc / Support

1 Combat Engineer — 90 ducats

Weapon: Heavy Shotgun, Trench Shield

Equipment: Engineer Body Armour, Combat Helmet, Shovel
(Utility/support unit with gear to handle objectives/explosives)


## Total

Total approximate cost: 120 + 60 + 140 + 150 + 130 + 90 = 700 ducats
